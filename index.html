What does Canadian Havyaka Association do?

Canadian Havyaka Association of Americas (CHAA) is a community organization of Indo-Canadians with their roots in southern part of India. The Goals of CHAA are: to educate, preserve and promote the valuable Havyaka (a sub sect of Hinduism) traditions, to inculcate good moral and cultural values in younger generation and make them valuable citizens of Canada.

CHAA's Community 

Since the beginning, CHAA has been keen on making the community bigger and stronger by always being very welcoming to new Havyaka's together. CHAA has over 300 members in its community, occasionally hosting many events at times of our cultural festivals and gather to celebrate as one community!
